CARD TYPE INFO

Rare (R)

Description: High chance of getting pulled, and anyone can join!
Chance: 70%
Supply: UNLIMITED

Super rare (sr)

Description: Slightly lower chances of getting pulled, and anyone can join!
Chance: 29.95%
Supply: UNLIMITED

Super super rare (ssr)

Description: These cards are created only upon request, and will generally have a reward when someone gets them. Usually these will be limited to 4 cards total, 5 cards total, etc.
Chance: 0.05%, Pity System
Supply: VERY LIMITED

Ultra rare (ur)

Description: These cards will only be created for gold sponsors and can only be pulled by using GOLD QR CODES. Gold QR Codes give 10 rolls. It will give 9 cards from the lower rarities, and a 100% chance of an ULTRA RARE CARD
Chance: 100% on gold QRs
Supply: UNLIMITED
