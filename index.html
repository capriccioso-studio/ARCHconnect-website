FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)

What exactly is the point of the App?

ArchConnect is all about fun and enhancing the ArchCon experience! Our aim is to provide participants with an interactive way to be part of the event, connecting with fellow attendees, artists, cosplayers, and tenants. We want to clarify that we're not using this platform for any data collection. It's purely a tool for enjoyment and community-building. Your participation makes ArchConnect come alive, and we're excited to share this fun experience with all of you at ArchCon!

What are the rights to the photos/data uploaded by the cosplayers?

NONE. We don't own anything the public uploads to our servers and are ready to remove them on request if they change their mind. This is simply a fun platform that uses public-sourced info and generates cards out of it to play in gacha, and this small Cebu studio has no intention of selling these cards as if they're our own.

What does Capriccioso gain from developing this app?

Nothing monetary. We earn zero pesos from this. We want to make that very clear. That said, ARCHcon is kind enough to provide us with a booth and x tickets that allow us to move freely in the venue and provide support for this game during the event.

We're developing ArchConnect to put our tiny game development studio on the radar and announce to the world our capabilities as a team. We essentially want to challenge ourselves as a team by taking on this huge responsibility!

We're joining ARCHcon for the same reasons as any congoer: have fun, meet the community, push beyond our comfort zone, and help out ARCHcon as a whole!

Lastly, the rewards or incentives (if any) of playing the Archconnect game will all be provided by the ARCHcon team or anyone who wants to do their own thing on our gacha platform. Capriccioso is not a sponsor, and will not sponsor for anything other than the development of this game.

TL;DR this is just exposure for Capriccioso

How is the RnG computed?

A number is generated on the server to determine which card rarity you'll get.

Afterwards, a 2nd number is generated on the server to determine which card under that rarity you will get.

These rng will be salted using a client nonce that we will not disclose and your userId. This is what the client will send to the server to generate the number.

What are the card's % rates?

Rare: 70%

Super Rare: 29.95%

Super Super Rare: 0.05%

Ultra Rare: 100% on gold QRs
